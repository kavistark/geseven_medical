Who We Are..?

GeSeven Medica, is a leading pharmaceutical company located in Petaling Jaya, Selangor. It was established in November 2007. The company’s principal business nature is to trade in medicines, medical devices, and medical equipment for clinics and hospitals. GeSeven Medica has been a well-known enterprise since its establishment and have been servicing private clinics and private hospitals in the Klang Valley. It was established by Mr Nadarajan Govindasamy, sole proprietor in Damansara Utama, Petaling Jaya who has successfully developed the business to approximately 500 customers as at 2013. He continues to apply his 20 years of experience in the medical fields to expand his business
